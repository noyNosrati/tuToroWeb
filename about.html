A Little About Me

My name is María Eugenia, I am the founder of tuToro Spanish course. I am a passionated Spanish teacher. I grew up in Venezuela. I did a degree in Spanish Literature at the UCV (Venezuela).

I worked as a Spanish teacher in Germany for 7 years. During this time I did a PhD in Hispanic Philology at the UNED in Spain. I am also a specialist in Adult Education and have a master’s degree in Distance Learning. In 1997 I founded my own Spanish school on Margarita Island in Venezuela. I have been teaching Spanish since 1992.

This knowledge, added to the experience of more than 20 years in teaching Spanish, is the basis of the Spanish Distance course that we offer today.

Being a teacher helped me understand that each student learns differently and has different life and learning rhythms. Distance learning courses allow students to organize their learning according to their own pace and time available.
